Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play Store for free. For desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to the Phantom app website.

Get Some SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $JEFFCTO. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy SOL from an exchange or cross-chain swap and send it to your wallet.

Go to Raydium or Jupiter

Go to raydium.io or jup.ag in Google Chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $JEFFCTO token address into Raydium or Jupiter and confirm the swap. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign it.

Switch SOL for $JEFFCTO

Switch SOL for $JEFFCTO. We have ZERO taxes, so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
